Policies

 

  • No smoking
  • Pet friendly with $100 per stay fee. Pets must be declared at the time of booking
  • The following breeds of dogs are not permitted: Dobermans, Chows, Rottweilers, Akitas, American Staffordshire Terriers (Pit Bulls), attack dogs and Wolf Hybrids, (or any mix thereof)
  • Max occupancy is 6. Exceptions can sometimes be made only with homeowner approval. If it is found that max occupancy is exceeded, an additional $100 fee will be imposed.
  • Additional fees and taxes may apply
  • Must be at least 25 years old to book
  • Photo ID may be required upon check-in
