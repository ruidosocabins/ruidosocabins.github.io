𝑻𝒉𝒆 𝑳𝒐𝒄𝒂𝒕𝒊𝒐𝒏

Ruidoso, New Mexico is known for many things, but particularly its serene mountain-village vibe among several large tourist destinations, such as the Ski Apache Ski Resort, Inn of the Mountain Gods Resort and Ruidoso Downs Horse Track, Home of the richest Quarter Horse Race on Labor Day Weekend.


The area is teeming with gorgeous alpine scenery and delicate wildlife, as well as numerous seasonal cultural events. Some of these events, such as the Aspencade Motorcycle Rally, will almost quadruple Ruidoso's usual population, and Ft. Stanton has a reenactment of 1800 fort life.

 

Whether your vacation interest is skiing, hiking, golf, or gambling at one of the local casinos, Ruidoso is guaranteed to have appeal to a wide range of travelers.
